Our Canadian
Firsts

Not a checklist to rush through — a gentle list of first memories Aman hopes to make with Shreya as Canada becomes home.

Why this exists: When Shreya moved to Canada, this became more than a travel project. It became a way to plan the first summers, first winters, first road trips, and first quiet weekends of your life together here.

First Big Water Moment

Tobermory, Indian Head Cove, or Sandbanks — somewhere the water makes the day feel unreal.

First Cottage Weekend

Muskoka or Haliburton: a cabin, a dock, a slow breakfast, and no rushing.

First Fall Colours

Algonquin, Muskoka, Mono Cliffs, or Dundas Peak when the trees look painted.

First Snow Day

Blue Mountain, Arrowhead, or a winter cabin weekend with hot chocolate and warm layers.

First Stargazing Night

Torrance Barrens on a clear new-moon night — blanket, tea, and no city lights.

First Anniversary Escape

Elora Mill, Niagara-on-the-Lake, or a PEC weekend saved for something special.
