Why it is worth considering

The hidden-gem angle

Devil’s Glen is best as an add-on, not a standalone weekend. It gives you gorge/valley scenery and a quieter nature feel before continuing to Collingwood, Blue Mountain, Creemore, or Wasaga-area plans.

Use it when you want the trip to have a nature anchor without committing to a huge hike.

Planning style: this is a hidden-gem page, so use it as a slower add-on or focused day rather than forcing it into a packed weekend.
