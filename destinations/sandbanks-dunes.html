Why it is worth considering

The hidden-gem angle

Sandbanks is best treated as a beach-day anchor inside a Prince Edward County trip. The dunes feel surprisingly different from the rest of Ontario, and the shallow water makes it a relaxed summer experience.

Because it gets busy, planning matters: book day-use early, arrive early, and avoid building a complicated itinerary around the same day.

Planning style: this is a hidden-gem page, so use it as a slower add-on or focused day rather than forcing it into a packed weekend.
