Why it is worth considering

The hidden-gem angle

Westport is for when you want the feeling of discovering something that most GTA weekend lists forget. It is small, walkable, lakeside, and calm.

The key experience is not rushing between attractions. Base the trip around Upper Rideau Lake, Foley Mountain / Spy Rock, a good dinner, and a slow village morning.

Planning style: this is a hidden-gem page, so use it as a slower add-on or focused day rather than forcing it into a packed weekend.

Best Experiences

Slow-travel priority order

Kayaking, boat watching, dock walks, and lakefront picnic mood.
