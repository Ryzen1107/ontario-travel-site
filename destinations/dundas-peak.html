Why this place belongs here

This guide focuses on places that are not just “nice,” but emotionally memorable — the kind of places where the drive feels justified the moment you arrive.

The feeling

Dundas Peak is not unknown, but it still belongs here because the view is genuinely breathtaking when the light and season are right.

The trick is to treat it as a timed experience: avoid the busiest moments, plan parking/reservations where required, and pair it with Dundas village or waterfalls rather than rushing through.

Best planning approach: do not stack too many stops. Let this place be the main event or pair it with one gentle food/small-town add-on.
