Why it is worth considering

The hidden-gem angle

Bonnechere Caves is worth considering when you want something unusual — not another lake, village, or waterfall. The draw is guided underground exploration and the feeling of stepping into a very old landscape.

Because it is a longer drive from Scarborough, this works better as part of an Ottawa Valley / cottage-country overnight rather than a rushed day trip.

Planning style: this is a hidden-gem page, so use it as a slower add-on or focused day rather than forcing it into a packed weekend.
