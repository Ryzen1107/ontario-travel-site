Stay guide

Glamping

Domes, tents, firepits, stars, and nature without giving up comfort.

❤️

Best for

First outdoor-style trips, stargazing weekends, and summer/fall nights with firepit energy.

💰

Budget strategy

Look for Sunday–Thursday discounts, off-season dates, and places where the stay includes the experience: dock, firepit, hot tub, or walkable town.

📸

Photo moments

Morning coffee, sunset on the deck, fireplace, outdoor tub, dock silhouettes, and one clean room/detail shot before unpacking.

What to check before booking

Distance

Confirm realistic drive time from Scarborough, not just the town name.

Food

Check vegetarian options nearby or plan a simple grocery stop.

Weather

For winter or remote stays, confirm road access and cancellation terms.
